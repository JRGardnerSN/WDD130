Overview

Purpose

Our mission is to share our thoughts, hopes, fears, and even the occasional joke or two. Too often we see people who shun or actively try to stop open discussion, so our goal is to make those discussions happen. Art, science, culture, history, food and just about everything in between are all part of the human experience; as for these idiots- we're still talking about one of those things.
